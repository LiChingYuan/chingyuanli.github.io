About 理念

_

_


      自高中美工科畢業，啟程了「南投水里蛇窯」 擔任陶瓷工藝師的30多個年頭。感謝蛇窯老闆林國隆的提攜，讓「水里蛇窯陶瓷工藝展」及「李清源陶藝個展」成為我日後發展的重要基石。隨後，我於「921災後社區重建」、「台灣茶業博覽會」、「高雄夢時代國際陶藝花器創作聯展」、「高雄陶藝文創協會中外藝品展」、「中國工藝美術大師暨國際藝術精品博覽會」、「冀台經貿文化博覽會」等展會與教學機會，積累豐厚經驗。「第七屆海峽兩岸廈門文化產業博覽會」榮獲【中華工藝精品獎 銀獎】所帶來的肯定更築起我返鄉造窯的夢想。

      一手成立的【李清源柴燒個人工作室】受到「世新新聞台」報導，為這片土地增添文藝價值。後續透過議員何子凡牽線，我與內人純慧攜手創作，於「竹崎文教基金會」展出「陶源彩繪夫妻聯展」，帶領家鄉朋友一同'陶'醉藝術! 感謝友人鍾瑩瑩、陳志弘、書法家吳國進等貴人一路相挺，創作過程從不孤單。

      我的創作園地坐落嘉義竹崎，除是對家鄉的情感，更盼作品能在大自然懷裡孕育，把一份純粹傳遞收藏者，感受作者與蟲鳴鳥叫、綠樹成蔭的對話。歡迎蒞臨藝術與自然交織的小天地，共賞純樸與悠然自得。若您意猶未盡，就用作品裝起空氣中的生意盎然，將這份美貯存起來吧!

       After completing the academy of Arts and Crafts in high school, my 3 decades journey of pottery art in Nantou Shuili Snake Kiln set off with passion. It was a privilege for me to be following in teacher GuoLong Lin 's footsteps as pottery craftsman. A series of exhibitions following "ChingYuan Li Pottery Art Solo Show" accumulated rich experience of mine and served as a stepping stone towards splendid future. Among those precious treasures, "The 7th Cross-Strait [Xiamen] Cultural Industry Fair" has become my turning point by awarding me Silver Award which light up my enthusiasm and the blueprint of kiln building in my hometown.

       Embracing clusters of lush green trees, "Ching Yuan Li Wood Fired Pottery Art Studio" is located in Jhuci Hsiang, Chiayi. Widely publicized by CYCCATV and Mr. He, a County council member, my artworks got a opportunity displaying at Jhuci Culture & Arts Foundation, inviting the visitors to raom about the beauty of pottery art in the name of "Pottery & Painting in Wonderland". Thanks to YingYing, ZhiHong, GuoJin and all of your appreciation, the stories of my creating is still writing new chapters in the womb of Mother Nature. While breathing in the aroma of soil and wind, your pupils may unconsciously fall in love with the sunlight and shade wiggling in the leaves curtain. Don't forget to feel the warmness that the golden sun shines sprinkled on your skin. Listen to the mumbling between insects and plants, and enjoy the process of soul purifying. Capture the moments with my artwork in case you forget how beautiful it was.

      May the goodness be recalled whenever you wanna immerse yourself into it.

Life is about Creating Experiences

      利用手拉坏技術結合手捏造型予以變化，採用柴燒技法燒製而成的作品在 落灰、火痕、結晶等自然紋理 的烙印下各自活成獨特樣貌，每次燒窯都像觀看神祕的魔術秀，直到出窯時刻，才能揭露當次的驚喜。因此柴燒雖然格外難掌控結果，我卻將它看作一場窯與陶的秘密對談，用心感受他倆為我的創作畫龍點睛。創作對我而言，就像柔軟土身滲進心靈、穿梭雙手並和構思相纏，身心手腦四合一的交織，匯成獨一無二的生命。柴火薰陶下，落灰和著熱氣披覆陶身，滲入裏內、桎梏住靈魂。至此，拾獲的大自然將不再無處安放，它們繼承創作者的一部分並活成千百種姿態，優雅倒映於有緣人的雙眸，在此綻放它鋒芒卻內斂的璀璨。

    Wheel Thrown Pottery, the eleganct Pirouette on a spinning wheel. Moisturized by water, pottery stretched its body smoothly in my palms. Getting taller and wider, it opened pores slowly and breathed in the morning mist. Suddenly, the dance moves stopped, and the patterns were put on as a wedding dress. The best part of pottery's life was starting then.
    Wood Ash Glaze, burning mark and crystallization seared onto the surface when woods and kiln roared fiercely at each other. The kiln played a misterious magic show, and I was the audience looking forward to unveil the ending. I grab a handful of Nature and turned it into a part of me, it shared my soul and kept my fingerprints inside the body. The bond between us would never change.
    Pottery was the reflection of my mind, it wrote down all my thought on itself. There's no word to describe the scene in the cup. There's no instrument can play the melody from the vase. It's impossible for teapots to duplicate the same flavor. Each human being is unique and so does my artwork.

       在生活中，發現美。 在美之中，找回生活。 Seek for beauty while living. Life was awakened at the mean time.
