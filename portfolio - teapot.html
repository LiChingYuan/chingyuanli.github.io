 teapot . [ 壺 ]

 



  茶的一生，陶肺腑裡沖泡 
  茶香滲透熱流，壺腹中翻湧 
  喚醒它誕世前 柴火中燒的記憶。 

  烈火 、冷卻 、注水 、溫熱 
  以茶載道  循環日常將記憶回甘現泡 
  品一口人生 啜飲風花雪月 

Brewing the tea in a teapot
Then the tea became teapot's lung

Breathing in and out
The fragrance penetrates into teapot's belly
blowing the tidal waves until it perfumed the air

Freash tea refresh one's memory
Recalling all the detail of my past
I am one with my teapot
I saw the firewood incubating the teapot
I heard the sound of flames, chill, water and heat
Having a pot of tea,
