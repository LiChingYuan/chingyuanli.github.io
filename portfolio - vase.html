 vase . [ 瓶 ]

 

  擷取  一株小植的青春 
  將它的璀璨時刻  送入陶瓶口中... 

  若  攝影是歲月和魔法簽下的契約 
  那麼瓶器  便是存放魔法金粉的腹囊    
  賦予懷中草花片刻永恆 

  凋零前 在舌尖上漫舞最美姿態 


The youth of a plant has been embroidered on the fabric of life,
woven with creepers and vines.

Its green limbs tangled up and then sank into the lips of the vase.
It was a secret place playing a romantic song,
and the stem was dancing with the beat.
Keep quiet so you can hear its steps.

Photographing is a contract signed between age and pixie dust,
while the vase is the sachet keeping the fairy tale.
It told a story about everlasting moments presented by petals and leaves,
wilt or not depends on your thought.
It may vary from person to person.

Now, it's your turn to tell a story yourself!
